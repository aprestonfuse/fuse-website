About the Firm

Purpose‑built for federal clients.

Fuse A&E is a full‑service architectural and multi‑disciplinary engineering firm specializing in the planning, design, and delivery of facilities and infrastructure for U.S. federal government agencies.

We support mission‑critical programs for the Department of Defense and federal civilian agencies through compliant, resilient, and mission‑ready design solutions delivered in regulated, security‑sensitive, and operationally complex environments.

Our work spans headquarters facilities, operational buildings, aviation infrastructure, secure environments, and specialized mission‑support spaces — domestically and internationally.

Our approach emphasizes disciplined execution, clear documentation, and principal‑level oversight under a single accountable A+E contract structure.

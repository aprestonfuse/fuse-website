About the Firm

A full-service A+E firm purpose-built for federal clients

FUSE A+E is a full-service architectural and multi-disciplinary engineering firm specializing in the planning, design, and delivery of facilities and infrastructure for U.S. federal government agencies.

We support mission-critical programs for the Department of War and other federal civilian agencies through compliant, resilient, and mission-ready design solutions — delivered under a single, accountable contract structure.

Our approach emphasizes disciplined execution, clear documentation, and principal-level expertise and oversight on every engagement.

About the Firm

Purpose‑built for federal clients.

Fuse A&E is a full‑service architectural and multi‑disciplinary engineering firm specializing in the planning, design, and delivery of facilities and infrastructure for U.S. federal government agencies.

We support mission‑critical programs for the Department of Defense and federal civilian agencies through compliant, resilient, and mission‑ready design solutions delivered in security‑sensitive and operationally complex environments.

Our model emphasizes disciplined execution, clear documentation, and principal‑level oversight — integrating architecture and engineering services under a single accountable contract structure.

Federal‑only practice focused on DoD and civilian agencies
Compliance‑driven delivery aligned with UFC and agency standards
Integrated A+E services under one contract
Domestic and international (CONUS / OCONUS) delivery
